Required Materials

Textbook

The required textbook is Learning Processing: A Beginner's Guide to Programming Images, Animation, and Interaction (2nd Edition, The Morgan Kaufmann Series in Computer Graphics) by Dan Shiffman.
We use this book because its emphasis on creative coding and visual thinking aligns well with our approach to programming in CS1.
It is available through the Purchase College Library, licensed for unlimited simultaneous users.
Simply follow the link, sign in with your Purchase credentials, and read online or download the book for offline use.

Troubleshooting

Occasionally, you may see the error “This site does not provide a secure connection.”

If that happens, try the following:

  • Try a different browser.
  • Clear your browser cache. If your browser asks what to remove, clearing cached files is usually sufficient.
  • Try an Incognito (Chrome) or Private (Safari) window.

If the issue continues, please contact me and we’ll sort it out together.
